Compressive Framework

Exploring Harmonic Coherence, Geometry, and Particle Dynamics

The Compressive Framework (CF) is an open scientific and computational model for studying resonance, field compression, and stability in multidimensional systems. Built collaboratively to advance safe, reproducible understanding of compressive and harmonic behavior.
